# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 14 April 2026  
**Last Updated:** 14 April 2026

## Privacy Policy for ParkMate

ParkMate ("**ParkMate**," "**we**," "**our**," or "**us**") provides a mobile app that helps users find parking zones, view and share parking availability reports, receive alerts, manage parking timers, and access related account features.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the ParkMate app and related services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Your login credentials and authentication data

### b. Location Information

If you allow location access, we may collect:

- Your device's approximate or precise location
- Location data used to show nearby parking zones, reports, and directions
- Location data used when you create, claim, or verify a parking report

### c. Parking Activity and User Contributions

When you use ParkMate features, we may collect:

- Parking spot reports you submit
- Spot claims and false-report actions
- Zone selections
- Activity feed and leaderboard-related data
- Reputation, points, trust, and contribution metrics associated with your account

### d. Notifications Data

If you enable notifications, we may collect and process:

- Push notification token(s)
- Device/session identifiers used to register notifications
- Notification interaction data

### e. Device, App, and Diagnostic Information

We may automatically collect:

- Device type
- Operating system
- App version
- Crash logs
- Error reports
- Performance and diagnostic information

### f. Locally Stored Information

The app may store information on your device, such as:

- Login session data
- Timer state
- Recently cached parking/report data
- Read/unread activity state
- App preferences

### g. Optional Permissions and Features

If enabled in your version of the app, ParkMate may request access to features such as:

- Camera
- Photo library
- Microphone
- Contacts

These permissions are only used if you choose to use the related feature.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate you and keep you signed in
- Show nearby parking zones and live parking reports
- Let you report, claim, delete, or flag parking spots
- Provide directions and map functionality
- Send parking alerts, timer reminders, and service notifications
- Display your profile, contribution history, and leaderboard position
- Improve app reliability, security, and performance
- Detect abuse, fraud, spam, or inaccurate reporting
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. How We Share Information

We may share information:

- With service providers that help us operate ParkMate
- With infrastructure, hosting, authentication, analytics, crash-reporting, notification, and mapping providers
- When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request
- To protect rights, safety, property, users, or the public
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets

We may also display certain account- or activity-related information to other users where relevant to app features, such as leaderboards, contribution history, or community parking activity.

## 4. Third-Party Services

ParkMate may rely on third-party providers such as:

- **Supabase** for authentication and backend services
- **Sentry** for crash and error reporting
- **Expo** and related notification services
- **Google Maps / Apple Maps** or similar mapping/directions providers

Your use of ParkMate may also involve those providers processing information under their own privacy terms.

## 5. Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to:

- Provide the service
- Maintain account functionality
- Support safety, moderation, and fraud prevention
- Meet legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory requirements
- Resolve disputes and enforce our terms

Locally stored information may remain on your device until deleted by you, the app, or your device settings.

## 6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Choices

Depending on your device, region, and applicable law, you may be able to:

- Access or update your account information
- Disable location permissions
- Disable notifications
- Delete the app and local app data
- Request deletion of your account and associated personal information by contacting us at support@parkmate.com

## 8. Children's Privacy

ParkMate is not intended for children under 13, or under the minimum age required in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. By using ParkMate, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date and may provide additional notice in the app or by other appropriate means.
